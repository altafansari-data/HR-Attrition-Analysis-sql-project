# HR Employee Attrition Analysis (SQL Project & Power BI Dashboard)

## Project Overview

This project analyzes employee attrition data using SQL to uncover key factors influencing employee turnover. The goal is to generate actionable insights for HR teams to reduce attrition and improve retention.

---

## Dataset

* HR Employee Attrition Dataset
* Contains employee demographics, job details, salary, and attrition status

---

## Objectives

* Identify attrition patterns across departments and job roles
* Analyze impact of salary, overtime, and experience
* Discover high-risk employee segments
* Calculate attrition rates for business insights

## Key Insights

* Employees working overtime show higher attrition
* Certain departments have significantly higher attrition rates
* Lower experience employees (<5 years) are more likely to leave
* Specific job roles dominate attrition trends
* Salary and job satisfaction play a key role in retention
